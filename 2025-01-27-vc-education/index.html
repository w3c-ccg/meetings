<phillip_long> Another method that Joe Andrieu has shared is Selective Redaction. It is different from Selective Disclosure. The former is to remove selected fields in a VC, whereas the latter is to derive a new Verifiable Presentation (VP) containing only the fields chosen for disclosure. Selective Redaction has the primary purpose of obfuscating selected fields in a VC, while still linking the VC to the original issuer.
