Nate Otto: Recipient push is another flow that should be represented, not just consumer/requestor pull ✪
Manu Sporny: https://dvcs.w3.org/hg/webpayments/raw-file/default/latest/use-cases/index.html#negotiation-of-payment-terms ✪
